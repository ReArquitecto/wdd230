Rye Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

Rye Chamber of Commerse is devoted to networking the buisnesses of Rye Colorado, allowing for buisnesses and contacts to find eachother and work together. The site presents contact information and valuable insight about the various buisnesses in the city.

Within the site there will be be a few different pages including Home, Discover, Directory, Join, and Contact pages

Domain Name

The domain name for the site will be ryechambercommerce.com

Our Audience

The Rye Chamber of Commerce serves a diverse group of businesses and professionals in the city of Rye and surrounding areas. Our members will target and include small business owners, entrepreneurs, and even Representatives from larger buisnesses. The site should appeal to anyone seeking to know more about the buisnesses here. For example:

Small business owners may like to see their buisnesses on the page and be able to see others. Entrepreneurs may find it useful to be able to find buisnesses to create relationships with to advance their own buisnesses. Representatives from larger corporations may want to see how to expand their reach and connect with smaller buisnesses.

Our goal is to connect and support these individuals and businesses, helping them to thrive and succeed in the local community.

Design

Logo:

The design of the Rye Chamber of Commerce website is modern, clean and user-friendly, creating an easy-to-navigate and visually appealing experience for our audience. The layout is designed to be responsive and accessible, ensuring that it can be viewed on any device and by people with disabilities.

Colors

Primary Color: #1e69b8
Secondary Color: #003f76
Accent Color: #f2f2f2
White: #FFFFFF
Gray: #333333

The color scheme is fairly small of two shades of blue, white and dark gray. There may be other colors used subtily throughout the site.

Typography

The font used throughout the site is Nunito, a modern sans-serif font. The font is clean and easy to read, and is used throughout the site. When this font is not working, the standard sans-serif font is used.

Layout

The layout of the site is designed to be simple and clean, following modern design guidlines, with a focus on the content. The navigation bar is fixed to the top of the page, allowing the user to easily navigate the site. The content is organized into sections, with a clear heading for each section.
