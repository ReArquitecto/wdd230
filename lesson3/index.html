Rye Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

Rye Chamber of Commerse is devoted to networking the buisnesses of Rye Colorado, allowing for buisnesses and contacts to find eachother and work together. The site presents contact information and valuable insight about the various buisnesses in the city.

Within the site there will be be a few different pages including Home, Discover, Directory, Join, and Contact pages

Our Audience

The Rye Chamber of Commerce serves a diverse group of businesses and professionals in the city of Rye and surrounding areas. Our members include small business owners, entrepreneurs, and representatives from larger corporations.

  • Small business owners
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Representatives from larger corporations
  • Professionals in various industries

Our goal is to connect and support these individuals and businesses, helping them to thrive and succeed in the local community.

Design

The design of the Rye Chamber of Commerce website is modern, clean and user-friendly, creating an easy-to-navigate and visually appealing experience for our audience. The layout is designed to be responsive and accessible, ensuring that it can be viewed on any device and by people with disabilities.

The color scheme is a combination of blue and white, with subtle use of other colors to accentuate important elements and sections, creating a professional and cohesive look. The typography is clear and easy to read, with a combination of serif and sans-serif fonts that are easy on the eyes. The images used on the site are high-resolution and relevant to the content, helping to enhance the user's experience.
